About Me

I began my career in the field of electrical engineering and programming when I was accepted into the Electrical Automation Engineering department at ITS. Since I started college, I have been focusing more on website programming, embedded systems, and PLCs. Moving on to the second semester, I learned a lot about electronics such as schematic design and PCB design, which is one of my interests in the field of electronics.
